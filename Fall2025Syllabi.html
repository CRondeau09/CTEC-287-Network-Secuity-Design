Course Focus

This course is aligned with CompTIA Security+ certification and prepares students for national cybersecurity standards.

Course Materials

All texts and labs are delivered through CompTIA CertMaster with 1 year of access for certification preparation.

Certification Goal

Students are expected to complete the CompTIA Security+ certification exam by the end of the semester.

Lab Requirements

37 total labs available - students must complete 20 labs minimum (all recommended for exam preparation).

Important: CompTIA Security+ Certification Exam is REQUIRED as part of your final grade.
Note: You have 1 year of access to CertMaster - save your links as Canvas access expires at term end.

Late Work Policy

Lose one letter grade per day late (up to 4 days)
After 4 days: Zero grade
Weekends count in deduction period

CertMaster to Canvas transfers may take up to 36 hours

Testing Procedures

Multiple choice, short answer, fill-in-blank, matching, true/false, and hands-on production tests

Attendance Policy

Attendance requirements and procedures

Face-to-Face Classes

3 tardies = 1 absence
Leaving early = absent for the day
Notify instructor by phone/email for planned absences

Online Classes

Complete course introduction assignment in first week
Weekly attendance through assignment submission
Must actively participate by start date

15% Rule

Missing 15% of course may result in College-Initiated Withdrawal with "WN" grade

No Show Policy

Must attend/participate by first class meeting
Online: Complete assignment within 24 hours
No Show = responsible for 100% tuition/fees

Instructor Policy: I DO NOT REMOVE SUSPENSIONS. Contact me beforehand for planned absences.
Excused Absences: School-sanctioned activities, military exercises, court appearances, or physician-documented conditions require official documentation.
